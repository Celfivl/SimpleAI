Au Poivre Sauce:
Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons shallots, finely chopped
- 1/4 cup brandy
- 1 cup beef broth
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 2 tablespoons coarsely ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- Salt to taste
Instructions:
1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Add shallots and cook until softened, about 3 minutes.
3. Add brandy and ignite with a match (optional). Let the alcohol burn off.
4. Add beef broth and bring to a simmer. Cook until reduced by half, about 5 minutes.
5. Stir in heavy cream, black pepper, and Dijon mustard.
6. Season with salt to taste.
7. Simmer for another 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.
8. Serve over steak or other grilled meats.
